NotesApp

Introduction

Many times when we use Notes taking app or TODO app the store data persistently in specific device storage. We cannot access it to other device and even if device lost or damaged data is lost, hence storing data on cloud can helps to access data on any device and any where.

Notes App uses Google signIn for authentication and firebase realtime Database. With a single account user can access his data any time and any where. and also it works offline.
